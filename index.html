The Break

Heartbreak

Do you know the feeling of heartbreak?
I think it’s improperly named
I gave you a piece of my heart but you didn’t shatter it
You just handed it back saying you didn’t want to carry around the weight anymore

It returned to me with an imprint of your finger
Making a small dent that was somehow big enough
to prevent the piece from fitting back into my heart
I had to sew it back in with strings of memories I cherish but need to let go of

But despite the stitches my heart didn’t ache
Without you stone cold,
it plummeted into my stomach
the impact crushing my hopes and appetite
The crash twisted my intestines like the butterflies used to do when I saw you
Except this time you weren't there to release them into the open air

A heartbreak doesn’t tear you from the inside out,
it slowly breaks you from outside in.
It manifests in the unconscious arm twitches
reaching out toward you when you’re not there
The pain spreads to the shoulders
making me feel like I am carrying a weight heavy enought to break my spine.
The pain shows itself in the white flashing heachaches
where my brain tries to remember something I so fiercely am trying to forget.

But my heart is still in its ice cold capsule
stubbornly refusing to feel a thing
A heartache cripples everything but the heart

Do you know the feeling of heartbreak?

what am i supposed to
complain about
when i’m the one
who cut out my heart
gave it to you
then stole it back
trying to shove the bleeding muscle
back into the hole in my chest
- Self inflicted wounds

i’m trying to remember all the nights
i questioned my worth
because of you
but all i remember
is missing you
- selective memory

i hate you
because a cute guy
a sweet guy
is holding my hand
but all i can think
is that his hand
doesn’t have enough calluses
for me to grip
like yours did
- i know you like the back of my hand

The Aftermath

after being scattered
i picked up the pierces
and sewed myself
stick by stitch
back together
after being blown to bits
the shrapnel is now infused in me
and i welded myself
burning and bleeding
back together now that’s it’s over
i’m stronger
but i could
never
do it
again

i’m still angry
that i let u
treat me the way you did
for so long
i can’t trust my judgment anymore
because for two years i judged you
to be innocent of any wrong
and myself to be guilty
of not being who you wanted me to be
i’m terrified
of loving again
because when i was with you
i hated who i became
i don’t want
to hate myself again
in order to love someone else
- i'm more important

The Doll

it’s my fault
that you love someone who does not exist
because i became the person
you’ve always wanted
but that person wasn’t me

i look back on the time
we were together
and i remember the girl
who was strong
but around you i turned weak
not weak in the knees like the fairy tales
just weak

i hate the weak girl
who you knew
she wasn’t real
you created her in your mind
and i just followed the code
she was a slave to your love
because she never knew someone could love
the idea of her
so much

i can tell what you want me to say
and it would be so easy
to whisper those words into your ear
to act like your marionette
doing what you want me to
you would love your doll
pamper her
she’s created in your image
she’s created in your imagination
because she doesn’t exist
i do
but
i can see the string you want to pull
would it be so bad
to just let you
mold me into someone who
you love ?

More than I Love You

this is the first time
i’ve said it
and meant it
with my entire being behind the words
trying to push them out harder
if only that would show you how much I do
the words alone aren’t powerful enough
three is too few
i want to say
you have the kindest soul and truest heart i have ever known
i want to say
you are the strongest man but it’s okay to be vulnerable i’ll hold you
i want to say
you make me completely comfortable to be myself
you don’t make me delusionally happy because the happiness you feed me is genuine i can feel it inside me warm and safe
i love you
this is the first time i said it
and meant more than just that
i didn’t know there was so much more
than i love you

Sculptor

i'm not religious
but if
God sculpted me
he must’ve created you
from the same clay
so that my head fits perfectly into the crook of your neck
your stomach fits the curve of my back
your arms the perfect size to wrap around my waist

i never knew
one person could hold a world
i thought that was a task saved only for gods
your strength comparable to titans
Atlas condemned but you think it’s a blessing
to hold mine in you hands
i never knew
one person could hold so much of me
in their mind
that they keep memories of the smallest things i like
where they could listen to the same story i've already told twice
and ask me to tell it again just to keep hearing my voice
i never knew there would exist a man
who could hold me in his arms
tell me that i’m caring
show me that i’m strong
intertwine our fingers, listen and fully understand
my whole world in the palm of my hand

The Greatest Poets

our love makes the great poets look dim
the love they speak of is merely infatuation
the poets speak of beauty
but the ugly parts of us are the strongest
we burned for each other but the skin healed and now the scars and bruises are just reminders of how hard we fell for each other
the poets speak of youth
but i’m in love with the wrinkles that will appear on your face decades from now
as our youth fades
our love will only age,
the poets eat grapes from a vine in their cloth but we let it sit, wine dripping from our lips
we taste just how sweet it can become
together all the tannins blossomed into memories of the life we shared with
when the wall paper starts chipping we will peel back revealing the mural painted on the wall a map of all the places we’ve been each a story we have told a thousand times but could tell a thousand more
the poets speak of radiance
but the golden eye of heaven was created from your smile and the heat from the way you look at me and the twinkle from your laugh and
they weren’t fortunate enough to hear it
the greatest poets know nothing
of me and you

it’s the quiet moments
the buzz of the screen
the light casting a perfect shadow on your profile
it’s your hand in mine
and the smell of your shirt
your nose buried in my hair as you breathe me in
it’s in the way that your eye crease
and the way i can finally breathe deep
when i sigh into your shoulder and all the knots dissolve
i don’t have to say
what it is
i know you know

The Puppeteer

In my eyes I am dancing on two crossed wooden beams
the sheer strings catch the light ever so perfectly that to me it seems
Like they shimmer, begging to be pulled
If temptation was a virtue, i would be made of gold

The light reflects your secrets, a tapestry of stories
I know how to make you smile and smooth away your worries
I can see who you want me to be
And I could become her so easily
The girl you desire, the one you dream of
Would it be so bad if I just let you mold me into someone who you love?

She's happy all the time, and shiny and new
she says all the right things at the right time and I would love her too
Shes made in your image
Shes made in your imagination
Because the girl you dreamed of is just a figmentation
So now who’s to blame? when the girl you love doesn't exist
You wanted me to be someone who I’m not, and I couldn’t resist
So did I play you or were you using me,
Maybe we were both filling voids we didn’t know were that deep
I just wanted to make you happy and now we are both in tears
But who do you cry to when you’re your own puppeteer?

Greed

if you strip me down past the bare bones
take away the physical and go straight for the soul
if you wash clear the fog of memories
you would be left with pure desire and need

I think maybe in another life i was happy just being happy but I can't picture that for me
There's a craving inside that i'm in love with
like content is a cage that I must break free
Because what am I without the yearning, the hope to be more?
I think I would go crazy behind white picket fences and closed doors.

They say greed is a sin but Id choose it over sloth
if I'm going to be a sinner i want to have the whole lot
give me everything from happiness to the pain
give me more than i can take, give me the credit and the blame

maybe it will finally satiate me
when i’ve run the world ragged, taken all the green
when i’ve experience everything in my short little life
when i can say i know everything but can’t remember the price

maybe it’s who i am
or who i need to be
so that i can feel like i'm alive
because what am i without the greed?
